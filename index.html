Avantikulam is a student-run society in our college dedicated to providing free education to aspiring students who wish to excel in competitive exams such as JEE, NEET, Olympiads, and other entrance tests. The society strives to bridge the gap for underprivileged and deserving students by offering high-quality study materials, mentorship, and guidance. Through collaborative efforts, Avantikulam aims to create an inclusive learning environment where students from diverse backgrounds can access the resources they need to succeed without any financial constraints.

The core mission of Avantikulam is to promote equal opportunities in education, empowering students to unlock their full potential. The society organizes regular sessions, mock tests, and doubt-clearing classes to ensure that the students are well-prepared for these tough exams. By engaging passionate student volunteers, Avantikulam creates a supportive community for students to thrive academically and reach their dreams of securing top ranks in national-level exams.
